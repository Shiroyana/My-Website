# Free Website Audit — Template

This is the actual deliverable behind "Get Your Free Website Audit." Fill
this out per prospect and send as a PDF or a short Loom-style walkthrough.
The goal: be specific and honest enough that it's clearly not a generic
template, without giving away so much that there's nothing left to sell.

Keep it to 1 page. If you can't fill in real numbers for a section, cut
the section rather than leaving it vague.
